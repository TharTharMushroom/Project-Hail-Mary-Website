What is the Hail Mary Mission?

Our sun is running out of energy because of Astrophage, and the Earth is in dire condition until this is rectified. The world's greatests scientests came together to give this planet a chance. The Hail Mary Mission is a one-way suicide mission on which three astronauts will investigate the star Tau Ceti to figure out why it is not affected by the Astrophage infestation. These brave astronauts will send the research they conduct back to Earth using revolutionary Beetle technology, which may be the only way to figure out how to prevent the sun from dimming.

The Spacecraft

The spacecraft, which costed trillions of dollars, is the most advanced invention mankind has ever developed. It harnesses the astrophage's unique energy storage to travel distances at speeds never believed to be possible. It is capable of long-duration isolated travel, and it features a centrifugal gravity system, a state of the art lab, and automated travel.

What are astrophage?

Astrophage are single-celled alien organisms that consume the energy of stars, causing the stars to cool and dim, which is why they are named after the Greek word for "star-eater". They absorb this energy in order to reproduce, using mitosis to create a copy of itself at the nearest planet with carbon dioxide. The Petrova line, a line of light that they emit from the sun to Venus, always stays at a wavelength of 25.984 microns and gives Earth the best indication of where astrophage are. More research into their incredible energy storage and heat resistance must be conducted.
